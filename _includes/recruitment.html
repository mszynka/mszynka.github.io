You might be wondering why there are job offers on my personal website. As part of my work and experience, I helped build what I believe is one of the best modern job platforms — the Solid Jobs job board. It was designed to improve transparency and give candidates more control throughout the hiring process. Every listing includes clear **salary ranges**, so you always know what to expect. You also get notified when employers interact with your profile, keeping you informed at every stage. The platform focuses on fairness, visibility, and a better overall experience for both candidates and companies. If you're exploring new opportunities in tech, this job board is a great place to start.
